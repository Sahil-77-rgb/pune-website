COLLEGES IN PUNE

1. MIT WPU Pune

MIT World Peace University (MIT WPU) in Pune is a premier institution known for its innovative approach to education and commitment to creating a peaceful world through holistic learning. Established in 1983, it offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, law, and liberal arts, among others. MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) is a university in Pune, India, that offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD courses. It has a 65-acre campus with industry-sponsored labs that promote cross-disciplinary research. MIT-WPU's research and development cell (RDC) connects researchers from different fields to promote new knowledge and tackle global challenges.

3. SYMBIOSIS

Symbiosis International University (SIU), located in Pune, is a renowned institution known for its emphasis on quality education and its commitment to promoting international understanding and cultural diversity. Founded in 1971, SIU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including law, business management, humanities, social sciences, and health sciences. Symbiosis International (Deemed University) or SIU was established in 1971 and is located in Pune. The university was granted ‘Deemed To Be’ status by the Ministry of Human Resource Development in 2002. SIU is awarded the Category-I status by UGC, and an 'A' grade by NAAC with a score of 3.58 on 4. Additionally, Symbiosis International (Deemed University) is also ranked 22 in the Indian Institutes category of Times Higher Education 2024 and ranked 32 in the University (overall) category of NIRF 2023.
